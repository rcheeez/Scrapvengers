How do we Work?

You schedule a pickup on our website or locate the nearest point to drop off the recyclables.

We come at your location and we weigh the collected recyclables and send you a confirmation via SMS or E-mail.

After collection and weighing the recyclables and supporting for a noble cause. You receive an acknowledgement for it.

After that your recyclables (plastic) are segregated into the categories of Hard Plastic & Soft PLastic

After segregating plastic recyclables, we sell those segregated plastics to many companies who recycle this plastic and save it from becoming a waste.

By Selling all the plastic recyclables to other comapanies, they recycle or reuse the plastic and make a product and circled up back to the economy.

About Us

We are SCRAPVENGERS!

Scrapvengers is a enterprise working towards a sustainable future by means of preventing all resources from getting wasted into landfills and oceans, by bringing it back to economy by recycling or reusing. Our aim is to build an ethical ecosystem by means of proper organised employment for waste workers.We ensure security of jobs and a good life for workers from all sections of the society.
